Core Department

Information Technology Department

The IT Department at TRX City serves as the digital backbone of the organization, responsible for the integrity, scalability, and security of the technological ecosystem that powers the Tun Razak Exchange.

This department provides the essential framework for digital innovation, data management, and infrastructure stability. It acts as the primary orchestrator for the information systems that connect our smart city initiatives with internal corporate efficiency.
