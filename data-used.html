Data Used in Organization

Analyzing the five critical data streams driving operations, resource tracking, and smart district management within TRX City Sdn Bhd.

Asset Data

One of the main types of data used in the organization is asset data, which is managed through the IT Asset Management System. This includes information about laptops, personal computers, servers, network equipment, and data center resources. Each asset is given a unique barcode so that it can be traced easily during check-in, check-out, auditing, and warranty tracking. This type of data helps the IT Department keep records accurate and up to date, while also making it easier to monitor hardware usage and asset movement.

Financial Data

Another important category is financial data, which is handled through the IFKA System. This data includes payments, invoices, financial records, and other transaction-related information used by the finance department. Because IFKA is described as the backbone of TRX’s business operations, financial data must be protected, accurate, and available for daily processing. The system also supports data recovery if errors occur during input, which shows that financial data is treated as a critical organizational resource.

Facility & Maintenance Data

TRX also relies on facility and maintenance data through the Facility Management System (FMS). This includes maintenance schedules, work orders, equipment status, and records of daily facility-related activities. The organization uses this data to ensure that operations run smoothly and that maintenance tasks are tracked properly. By keeping detailed records of maintenance work, TRX can reduce disruptions, plan repairs more effectively, and maintain a safe and efficient working environment.

Human Resource Data

In addition, the organization uses human resource data through the Employee Claim System (ESS). This includes employee claim submissions, holiday claims, approval status, and related HR records. Such data helps the HR department manage employee requests more efficiently and maintain accurate personnel records. Since the system is used for routine employee transactions, the data must be organized and accessible so that claims can be processed without delay.

Real-Time Ops Data

For district operations, TRX uses real-time operational data through the TRX Integrated Management System. This data comes from building automation, parking guidance, traffic management, tunnel control, and smart sensors. It helps district management monitor conditions in real time and respond quickly to alerts or emergencies. Because TRX operates as a smart district, this type of data is essential for safety, coordination, and service optimization.
