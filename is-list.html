Technology Stack

List of Information Systems

An overview of the core software infrastructures and digital applications identified during our interview session at TRX City Sdn Bhd.

System 01

IT Asset Management System

Overview: Tracks and manages all physical IT assets (laptops, servers, network gear, rented data‑center resources) using unique barcodes and daily updates.

Core Functions

  • Barcode-based lifecycle tracking (register, scan, update).
  • Check-in/check-out tracing to locate assets and users.
  • Exportable reports for inventory, audits, and utilization.
  • Warranty tracking to avoid coverage lapses.

System Objectives

  • Keep accurate, up‑to‑date IT inventory.
  • Simplify and speed audits.
  • Prevent asset loss via traceability.
  • Ensure warranty compliance and cost control.
System 02

IFCA Application

Overview: Core financial system for TRX City—processes payments, invoices, P&C data, and supports error recovery for reliable finance operations.

Core Functions

  • Process accounts payable, receivable, general ledger entries.
  • Manage Property & Casualty financial data.
  • Data-recovery/error-revert capability for incorrect entries.
  • Generate financial reports for day‑to‑day operations.

System Objectives

  • Centralize and simplify financial processing.
  • Maintain accurate financial records.
  • Enable error recovery to protect data integrity.
  • Keep financial workflows organized and efficient.
System 03

Facilities Management System (FMS)

Overview: Manages maintenance tasks and schedules across TRX facilities to ensure continuous, efficient facility operations.

Core Functions

  • Create and track maintenance work orders from request to completion.
  • Schedule preventive maintenance across facilities.
  • Provide visibility of pending/completed tasks and maintenance history.
  • Produce reports on costs, equipment performance, and recurring issues.

System Objectives

  • Ensure uninterrupted facility operations.
  • Prevent equipment failures via proactive maintenance.
  • Improve long‑term planning and budgeting.
  • Centralize facility activity records for managers.
System 04

Employee Self-System (ESS)

Overview: HR system for digital submission and approval of employee claims and holiday requests, replacing paper workflows.

Core Functions

  • Submit and track claims/leave electronically.
  • Automated approval workflows for managers.
  • Maintain accurate leave balances and claim histories.
  • Provide employee visibility of claim status.

System Objectives

  • Streamline claim and leave processes.
  • Reduce paper-based HR administration.
  • Keep accurate employee records.
  • Improve HR efficiency and employee experience.
System 05

TRX Integrated Management System

Overview: Smart district command platform integrating Building Automation, Parking Guidance, Traffic Management, and Tunnel Control for real‑time monitoring and automated safety responses.

Core Functions

  • Building Automation: monitor elevators/escalators and send real‑time fault alerts.
  • Parking Guidance: show available spaces and guide drivers.
  • Traffic Management: monitor and act to improve ground‑level traffic flow.
  • Tunnel Control: detect fire/flood, trigger automatic safety measures and alerts.

System Objectives

  • Centralize district operations and control.
  • Enable real‑time monitoring and automated incident responses.
  • Improve public safety with automatic alerts/measures.
  • Reduce manual labour and enhance visitor experience.
