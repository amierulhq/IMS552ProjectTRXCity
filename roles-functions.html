IT Department

Roles & Functions

The IT Department serves as the digital backbone of TRX City Sdn Bhd, ensuring all systems, networks, and software operate efficiently, securely, and effectively to support organizational goals.

Core Roles

  • Manage and maintain the organization’s information systems and digital infrastructure.
  • Support communication and data sharing between departments.
  • Ensure cybersecurity, data protection, and system security.
  • Monitor system performance and resolve technical issues.
  • Support smart building technologies and digital operations.
  • Assist employees with technical support and IT-related services.

Key Functions

  • System administration and network management.
  • Database and information management.
  • Software and hardware maintenance.
  • Cybersecurity monitoring and risk management.
  • Technical support and troubleshooting.
  • Implementation of new technologies and digital solutions.
  • Backup, recovery, and data protection management.
Operational Impact

The IT Department contributes significantly to improving operational efficiency, decision-making, and digital transformation within the organization.
