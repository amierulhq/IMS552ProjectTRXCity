About Our Project

Exploring Information Systems Management at TRX City Sdn. Bhd.

This project aims to analyze the implementation and management of information systems within TRX City Sdn Bhd, the master developer of Tun Razak Exchange (TRX), Malaysia’s international financial district. Through research, observation, and interview sessions conducted with the organization, this project explores how technology, digital infrastructure, and information systems support organizational operations, communication, and strategic decision-making. The study also examines the effectiveness, challenges, and future opportunities of information systems in supporting a modern smart-city development environment.

This web-style presentation provides an interactive overview of the organization, its information systems, operational structure, and digital management practices at TRX City Sdn. Bhd. to streamline development frameworks.
