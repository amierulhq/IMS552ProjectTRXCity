Corporate Identity

Strategic Framework

The core foundational pillars directing operational blueprints, systems development, and infrastructure scaling inside TRX City Sdn Bhd.

Corporate Vision

To establish TRX as a world-class international financial and business hub that promotes sustainable urban development and global investment opportunities.

Corporate Mission

To develop and manage a smart, sustainable, and connected financial district that enhances economic growth, investor confidence, and modern urban living in Malaysia.

Key Objectives

Actionable Strategic Milestones

To position Kuala Lumpur as a leading international financial centre.
To attract local and foreign investments.
To support digital transformation and smart infrastructure.
To provide sustainable and future-ready urban development.
To improve connectivity, business opportunities, and community lifestyle.

Long-Term Goals

Ultimate Mission Outcomes

Strengthen Malaysia’s global economic competitiveness.
Deliver efficient and innovative infrastructure development.
Create a vibrant and sustainable business ecosystem.
Promote environmental sustainability and ESG practices.
Enhance operational efficiency through information systems and technology.
