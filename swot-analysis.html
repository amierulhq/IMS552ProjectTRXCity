Strategic Evaluation

SWOT Analysis

Assessing the internal capabilities and external environmental factors impacting the IT Asset Management System at TRX City Sdn Bhd.

SWOT
S

Strengths

  • Reduce manual workload through the use of barcodes.
  • Improve audit accuracy and accountability of asset entry and exit controls.
  • Facilitates warranty monitoring and hardware lifecycle planning.
W

Weaknesses

  • It depends heavily on staff discipline to update data.
  • The risk of human error affects the accuracy of records.
  • Lack of integration with other systems, affecting reporting efficiency.
O

Opportunities

  • Increase asset visibility for future hardware replacement budget forecasting.
  • Strengthen company technology audit compliance levels to reduce losses.
  • Maximize warranty monitoring to avoid unexpected maintenance costs.
T

Threats

  • Risk of cyber attacks or data breaches on asset central databases.
  • The rapid evolution of technology that can quickly render existing systems obsolete.
  • Discontinuation of security update support by external software vendors.
