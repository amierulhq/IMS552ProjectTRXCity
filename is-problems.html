System Vulnerabilities

Information System Problems

Although the IT Asset Management System simplifies daily operations through individual barcodes, the IT Department still faces several major challenges that risk the validity of reference data.

Human Error & Dependency

The most significant problem is that the system is too dependent on staff discipline to enter data and perform scans consistently. If staff forget or delay updating records during the hardware check-in/check-out process, the information in the central database becomes inaccurate and outdated.

Integration Silos

Problems arise if the system is not well integrated with other support systems or departmental systems at TRX City Sdn Bhd. This lack of seamless integration causes the monitoring and cross-reporting process to be inefficient, forcing the IT Department to manage asset reporting separately.

Recovery & Correction Delays

The final challenge occurs when a critical system error or data entry error happens. Even though there is a data recovery plan via data center backups, the process of re-verifying, manually correcting data, or reverting data still takes time and disrupts the smooth monitoring of daily operations.

Operational Efficiency

Performance of Information System

The performance of the ITAMS is assessed based on its ability to process inputs, carry out backend activities, and produce the necessary outputs required by the IT Department.

1. Input

The system functions well in receiving various important data streams efficiently:

  • Asset type & barcode number
  • Location & department details
  • Responsible user name
  • Warranty status & access control details

2. Processing

All input data is processed centrally to ensure records are always properly aligned:

  • Centralized database processing
  • Supported by company IT infrastructure
  • Powered by physical servers
  • Backed by stable data center resources

3. Output

The system efficiently produces outputs that are highly helpful to management:

  • Periodic audit reports
  • Downloadable asset lists
  • Traceability records
  • Latest warranty status reports
Overall Performance: Satisfactory Level

With this systematic output, the overall performance of technology inventory management at TRX City Sdn Bhd allows asset lifecycle monitoring to be executed more systematically, completely eliminating the need to rely on outdated, physical paper records.
